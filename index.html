The Pizza Game

1. App Representation

The Pizza Game is a casual/arcade game where players throw pizza slices to score points and unlock new pizza recipes. The goal is to complete pizzas and earn coins to access more recipes and improve the gaming experience.

2. App Functionality

Game Features:

  • Throw pizza slices: Players throw pizza slices at targets to earn coins.
  • Earn more coins: The longer the streak, the more coins players earn.
  • Unlock new pizzas: Use coins to unlock over 30 new pizza recipes.
  • Check ingredients: View the ingredients of each unlocked pizza.

3. Data Usage Transparency

The application does not collect any personal data directly from users. However, it uses third-party services such as Google Play and AdMob, which may collect data like IP address, app usage time, and device information. This data is processed anonymously and is not shared with us.

Third-party services used:

  • Google Play Services: Collects device and interaction data.
  • AdMob: Collects data for personalized ads.

These data are managed and stored by third-party services, not by FractalTeamGames.
